How access works

Users sign in with username, password, and class code. The system opens the correct workspace based on the account role and class assignment.

  • Students submit reports to a chosen instructor from the same class.
  • Instructors can open uploaded files, review content, and send decisions.
  • Admins monitor users and reports across the system.
